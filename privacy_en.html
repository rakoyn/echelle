Definition of Personal Data
In this document, the term "personal data" refers to all identifying information, such as full name, address, email address, phone number, gender, and date of birth, which are voluntarily provided when filling out the contact form.
Additionally, through cookies and other technological tools (such as web analytic tools), certain information may be automatically collected, including IP address, browser type, unique device identifier, browser characteristics, operating system, language preferences, referring URLs, and other technical data related to website usage.

Use of Cookies and Tracking Technologies
We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us:
a. Keep your session active
b. Remember your preferences
c. Analyze website performance
d. Personalize content based on your interests
You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings.

Purposes of Personal Data Processing
Your personal data is processed for the following purposes:
a. To communicate with you and respond to your inquiries or comments
b. To improve your browsing experience on the website
c. To analyze website traffic and enhance our services
d. To verify your age, as the website is intended for users over 18 years old

Protection and Security
We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data. Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel bound by confidentiality obligations.
Security measures include:
a. Data encryption during transmission
b. Regular security assessments
c. Intrusion detection and prevention systems
d. Regular data backups
e. Secure access protocols

Your Rights
You have the following rights regarding your personal data:
a. Right to access your data
b. Right to rectify inaccurate data
c. Right to erasure
d. Right to restrict processing
e. Right to data portability
f. Right to object to processing
g. Right to withdraw consent
h. Right to restriction of processing
i. Right to object to processing

Policy Modifications
We reserve the right to modify this Policy whenever necessary. Any significant changes will be communicated through the Website. We recommend visiting this page regularly to stay informed about any updates.

Data Retention
We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy or as required by law. After this period, your data will be deleted or anonymized.

Minor Users
Our website is not intended for individuals under 16 years old, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from someone under 16, we will take steps to delete such information.

Data Transfers
We may transfer your data to third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website. All service providers are contractually bound to protect your data and use it only for specified purposes.

Contact
For any questions regarding the processing of your personal data or to exercise your rights, you can contact us via the contact form on our Website. You also have the right to file a complaint with the Data Protection Authority ([www.dpa.gr](http://www.dpa.gr)).
